Bolognese

Description

Bolognese, originating from Bologna, Italy, is a rich meat sauce made with ground beef, tomatoes, onions, and garlic, simmered to perfection. This hearty sauce, filled with aromatic herbs and spices, clings deliciously to pasta. It's the epitome of Italian comfort food, offering a taste of home-cooked warmth in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces/400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup beef or vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 1 pound (450g) cooked pasta (such as spaghetti or fettuccine)
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese, for serving
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish (optional)

    • Instruction

    1. Brown the Meat:
      • In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
      • Add ground beef and cook until browned. Remove excess fat if necessary.
    2. Add Aromatics:
      • Add chopped onions and minced garlic to the skillet.
      • Saut√© until onions are translucent and garlic is fragrant.
    3. Tomato Base:
      • Stir in crushed tomatoes and tomato paste. Mix well to combine.
      • Pour in beef or vegetable broth and red wine (if using).
    4. Season and Simmer:
      • Add dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and black pepper.
      • TLower the heat and let the sauce simmer for at least 20-30 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
      • If the sauce thickens too much, add a bit more broth.
    5. Cook the Pasta:
      • While the sauce simmers, cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente.
      • Drain.
    6. Combine and Serve:
      • Add the cooked pasta to the Bolognese sauce.
      • Toss well to coat the pasta evenly with the sauce.
    7. Serve and Garnish:
      • Serve the Bolognese pasta hot, garnished with freshly grated Parmesan cheese.
      • Garnish with a few fresh basil leaves for a burst of color and freshness if desired.

    Enjoy your delicious homemade Bolognese pasta!!

Lasagna

Description

Lasagna, an Italian classic, is a delightful medley of flavors and textures. Layers of tender pasta, savory marinara sauce, creamy béchamel, seasoned meats, and melted cheese come together in perfect harmony. Baked to golden perfection, it is a comforting dish that embodies the essence of home-cooked warmth and shared meals, making it a timeless favorite enjoyed by families and friends around the world.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 1/2 pound (225g) Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces/800g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces/170g each) tomato paste
  • 2 cans (14 ounces/400g each) tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt, divided
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper, divided
  • 3 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

For Béchamel Sauce

  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instruction

  1. Cook the Lasagna Noodles:
    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    • Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the Meat Sauce:
    • In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onions and garlic, sauté until softened.
    • Add ground beef and Italian sausage, cook until browned and no longer pink. Drain excess fat.
    • Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, water, sugar, basil, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon black pepper. Simmer the sauce for 20-30 minutes.
  3. Prepare the Béchamel Sauce:
    • In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Stir in flour to create a roux.
    • Gradually whisk in milk, cooking and stirring until the sauce thickens.
    • Add nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Remove from heat and set aside.
  4. Prepare the Ricotta Filling:
    • In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, beaten egg, chopped parsley, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon black pepper. Mix well.
  5. Assemble the Lasagna:
    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
    • Place three lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
    • Spread a third of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by a third of the béchamel sauce and a third of the mozzarella cheese.
    • Repeat the layers twice, ending with mozzarella cheese. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top.
  6. Bake the Lasagna:
    • Cover the dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
    • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25-30 minutes, or until the lasagna is hot and bubbly, and the cheese is golden brown.
    • Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving to allow the layers to set.

Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
